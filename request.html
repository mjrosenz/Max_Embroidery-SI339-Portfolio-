request

a few things to consider before requesting:

Make sure to say the colors you would like to include in your design. Sometimes, I only have a limited amount of colors, and I may ask you to pay a fee for a new color if necessary.

Be as specific as possible! This will help me make sure I can fit your needs and present you with something you'll enjoy.

Give me a time frame. Embroidering takes a whole lot of time. I want to give you your commission on time!
