about me

Hi, I'm Max Rosenzweig!

I am a student at the University of Michigan (Class of 2023) who recently got into embroidery! I hail from West Bloomfield, Michigan and currently study User Experience Design and Research at the School of Information.

why embroidery?

I chose to learn embroidery because I have always loved the meticulousness that goes into every design. After high school, I struggled to find a creative outlet. During my school quarantine in September, however, I decided to teach myself.

catharsis

Embroidery is such a therapeutic hobby for me. I can sit down and focus deeply on threading each section of my design. It's quite meditative. I embroidered my most recent design while I rewatched the entirety of the first two seasons of iCarly.
